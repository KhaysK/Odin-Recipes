Lasagna recipe

Back to main page

Description

This lasagna recipe is a fun dish and I guarantee everyone will love it. So try it and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 2 (1.25 ounce) packages taco seasoning mix
  • 2 (1.25 ounce) packages taco seasoning mix
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • ½ cup water
  • cooking spray
  • 18 (6 inch) corn tortillas
  • 1 (24 ounce) jar salsa
  • 1 cup sliced green onion
  • 1 (16 ounce) container sour cream
  • 1½ cups shredded Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Place ground beef in a large, deep skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until evenly browned. Drain, then season with taco seasoning, garlic, chili powder, and cayenne pepper. Pour in water. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  3. Place 6 tortillas into the prepared baking dish. Spread 1/3 of the salsa on top of the tortillas. Spread 1/2 of the meat mixture evenly over the salsa. Sprinkle with 1/2 of the green onions. Drop 1/2 of the sour cream randomly over the green onions. Repeat layers. Top with 6 tortillas, spread with remaining salsa, and sprinkle with remaining cheese.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 45 minutes or until cheeses are melted.
